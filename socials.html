Summary About Me

Hi! I'm a second-year Software Engineering student at Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM), currently in my second semester.
I'm from Terengganu, and I enjoy learning new things about tech and design. Studying here has really opened my eyes to
how fun and creative software development can be.

I’ve been learning Java, Python, C++ and HTML, and I especially like working on the front-end side of websites. I love

confident in my coding and design skills.

One day, I hope to become a UI/UX designer who creates websites and apps that are both beautiful and helpful. I’m always
curious and trying to improve my skills even in the future. For now, I’m just enjoying the journey and passionate to
learn more about my course while seeing where it takes me!
